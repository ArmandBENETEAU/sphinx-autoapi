

DefaultControllerTypeProvider Class
===================================



.. contents:: 
   :local:



Summary
-------

A [Microsoft.AspNet.Mvc.IControllerTypeProvider](Microsoft.AspNet.Mvc.IControllerTypeProvider.yml) that identifies controller types from assemblies
specified by the registered [Microsoft.AspNet.Mvc.IAssemblyProvider](Microsoft.AspNet.Mvc.IAssemblyProvider.yml).
