

CompositeValueProvider Class
============================



.. contents::

Summary
-------

Represents a [Microsoft.AspNet.Mvc.ModelBinding.IValueProvider]() whose values come from a collection of [Microsoft.AspNet.Mvc.ModelBinding.IValueProvider]()s.
