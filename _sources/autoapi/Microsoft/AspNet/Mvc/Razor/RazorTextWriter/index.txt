

RazorTextWriter Class
=====================



.. contents:: 
   :local:



Summary
-------

A [System.IO.TextWriter]() that is backed by a unbuffered writer (over the Response stream) and a buffered
[Microsoft.AspNet.Mvc.Rendering.StringCollectionTextWriter](). When
<c>Flush</c> or
<c>FlushAsync</c> is invoked, the writer
copies all content from the buffered writer to the unbuffered one and switches to writing to the unbuffered
writer for all further write operations.
