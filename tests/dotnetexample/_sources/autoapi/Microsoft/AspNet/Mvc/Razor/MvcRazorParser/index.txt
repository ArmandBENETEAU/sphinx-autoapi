

MvcRazorParser Class
====================



.. contents:: 
   :local:



Summary
-------

A subtype of @!:RazorParser- that [Microsoft.AspNet.Mvc.Razor.MvcRazorHost](Microsoft.AspNet.Mvc.Razor.MvcRazorHost.yml) uses to support inheritance of tag
helpers from
<c>_ViewImports</c> files.
